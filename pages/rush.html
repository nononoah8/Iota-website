Why Rush Iota?

Professional Growth

From professional development workshops to industry guest speaker sessions, Iota Omega Epsilon prioritizes providing resources for members to succeed in their career endeavors.

Brotherhood

Iota holds many social events each semester, and the community is close-knit. You will meet brothers who not only may share your professional goals but become your closest friends!

Networking

In addition to networking workshops and events, our network of brothers pursue all kinds of majors and careers, from healthcare consulting to engineering. We help each other and grow together.

Career Opportunities

At Iota Omega Epsilon, we strive to open career opportunities for members. Through our project teams, career treks, and much more, brothers can both explore their passions and succeed.
